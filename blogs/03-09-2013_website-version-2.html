Website version 2

September 3rd, 2013

As can clearly be seen, this website has been completely redesigned. The content is still the same though. We felt that the old design was too heavy and dark, and felt boring.

Therefore, we tried with this new theme a much more light approach, built with the excellent (and free!) Bootstrap. Bootstrap makes life a lot easier when designing a website, by solving a lot of cross-browser issues and speeding up the development process by providing a template.

Our website is based on a Bootstrap example called Narrow Jumbotron, although we've made our own changes to approach a more minimalistic look and change the header.

You can write to us or start a discussion on Facebook, Twitter, Google+ and LinkedIn. Just use the links on your screen. Drop your opinion about the new layout and design, or just send us a thought.
