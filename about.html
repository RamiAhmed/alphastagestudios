About Alpha Stage Studios

Alpha Stage Studios is a small indie game company founded by two students from Aalborg University in Copenhagen, Denmark. The company was founded in 2013 with literally no budget or backing of any kind. Alpha Stage Studios' first game project was a standalone version of the popular Warcraft 3 custom map "Legion TD".
