About Alpha Stage Studios

Alpha Stage Studios was founded in Copenhagen, Denmark, in July 2013. The company was founded by two students from Aalborg University in Copenhagen, studying on their bachelor semester.

Alpha Stage Studios is an indie studio specializing in games, but also takes orders on apps or websites.
