Right To Rule

Right To Rule is a unit-based defense game (like tower defense, but with movable units). Right to Rule currently features a functioning Tactical AI System (TAIS), allowing players to set tactics on their units to influence their behaviour in combat. Right to Rule is powered by Unity3D.
